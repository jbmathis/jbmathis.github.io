About Me

As an avid reader, marching band enthusiast, and interaction designer, I've always been fascinated with different kinds of communication. Whether it be through written word, music, or digital interfaces, I love exploring, creating, and sharing ideas in meaningful ways. Simply communicating ideas is not enough though. I want to address real problems in society and communicate solutions that will engage communities across the world.

This ambition led me to the University of Michigan School of Information, where I currently study user experience design. My experience in business, psychology, design, and programming allows me to understand constraints from all of these areas and address them during the design process. I am a strong communicator who can foster collaboration across departments to ensure that we create a product that will positively impact people's lives.
