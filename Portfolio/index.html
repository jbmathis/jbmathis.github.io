Everyone has a story. Here is mine:

Stories have long been my obsession - a great book, addicting video game, or compelling song can easily pull me in. Effective storytelling can inspire people to consider new viewpoints, find a community, and achieve their goals.

As a UX professional, my passion is rooted in understanding user’s stories and crafting a delightful experience that incorporates them.

I was introduced to UX at the University of Michigan School of Information, where I received my bachelor’s degree in UX design. I currently work at General Motors as a UX analyst who influences their enterprise IT tools.
